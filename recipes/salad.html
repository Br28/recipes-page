Salad Dressing

Description

Salad dressing is a mixture of oil, vinegar, herbs, and other flavourings, which you pour over a salad. ... low-calorie salad dressings

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Green Beans
  • Lettuce

Steps for preparing a salad dressing

  1. Place Dressing ingredients and Optional Flavourings in a jar and shake well until mustard dissolves.
  2. Makes enough for a big leafy salad for 4 people (4 big handfuls of lettuce), or garden salad.
  3. Storage: Excluding garlic and fresh herbs, Dressing will keep for weeks in the fridge - just give it a good shake before using. Big batch
  4. Use to dress any fresh salad (make sure leaves are dry otherwise it won't stick) or steamed, boiled or roasted vegetables (douse while hot so it absorbs!). Drizzle and toss well just before serving.
