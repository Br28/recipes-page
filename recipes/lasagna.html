Lasagna

Description

Lasagna (plural “lasagne”) is a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese), Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce).

Ingredients

  • Dry sheets of pasta
  • Meat Ragu
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzerella

Steps for preparing Lasagna

  1. Heat oven to 400F
  2. Brown the beef and onion
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna for an hour
  6. Sprinkle the remaining Mozzerella
  7. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes
