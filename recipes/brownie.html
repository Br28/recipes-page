Brownies

Description

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa
  • Syrup
  • Corn Starch
  • Salt
  • Palm Oil

Steps to make brownies

  1. Prep chocolate or Cocoa
  2. Melt Cocoa and butter
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Add Milk and eggs
  5. Add Flour
  6. Bake,cool and then frost
